Read the base first

A scent unfolds in three stages: top (first minutes), middle (the heart), base (what lingers for hours). You live with the base longest — so it weighs most in your match score.

One trick

Choose Extrait de Parfum or rich EDP over EDT. More concentration means less alcohol sting and stronger projection — your "strong but smooth" sweet spot.
